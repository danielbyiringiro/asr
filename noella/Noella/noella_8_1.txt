abandi makumyabiri na bo barukuweho mu mpera z’iki cyumweru. Ibi biratuma hasigara cumi n'umwe barimo bane bakomoka
muri Iran, babiri bo muri Albania, umwe wo muri Syria, babiri bo muri Iraq. Abandi ntihahishuwe ubwenegihugu.
